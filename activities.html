What can we do in Denmark?

Fun activities to do!

1 / 10

Stargazing

If you want to stargaze, Dark Sky Park is the most suitable place to do it. In Dark Sky Park, you can experience the Dark Sky phenomenon. Furthermore, you can experience the clear and densely packed starry sky above Møn and Nyord and observe many shootings star crossing the Milky Way.
Name of the Place: Dark Sky Park
Address: Nordgade 7 Nyord, 4780 Stege, Denmark
Operating Hours: Opens daily, 6:00 p.m - 11:00 p.m

2 / 10

Hot Air Balloon

Hot Air Balloon
You can ride hot air balloons to enjoy the sunrise and sunset above Denmark. It is safe, does not require any physical strength and brings memorable vacations for you. You can book hot air balloons ride at Dreamballoon Aps. Before booking a hot balloon ride, please check the weather forecast first because weather in Denmark is unpredictable.
Name of the Place: Dreamballoon Aps
Address: Svejsegangen 8, 2690 Karlslunde, Denmark
Operating Hours:
-Monday to Friday, 10:00 a.m. – 1:00 p.m.
-Saturday to Sunday, 2:00 p.m. – 3:00 p.m.
Take Me There

Cycling

Cycling
Cycle paths is everywhere in Denmark, but you should visit Bornholm and Svenborg. In Svenborg, cyclists are allowed to discover different areas. If you are a sea lovers, you should cycle along the coast and while culture enthusiasts should head north and visit Egeskov. You can rent a bicycle at Svendborg Cykeludlejning.
Name of the Place: Svendborg Cykeludlejning
Address: Frederiksø 18F, 5700 Svendborg, Denmark
Operating Hours: Tuesday to Sunday, 9:00 a.m - 2:00 p.m

Canal Tour

If you want to join cozy canal tour, you can book at Stromma Canal Tours Copenhagen to enjoy a classic canal tour around Copenhagen’s harbour and the idyllic canals. This boat sightseeing tour departs from Nyhavn and Ved Stranden and passes some of Copenhagen's best sights and attractions. For instance, The Little Mermaid, the Old Stock Exchange, the Opera, the Black Diamond and Christiansborg.
Name of the Place: Stromma Canal Tours Copenhagen
Address: Nyhavn 3, 1253 København, Denmark
Operating Hours: Opens daily, 10:00 a.m - 5:10 p.m
