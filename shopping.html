Famous Shopping Destination in Denmark

1 / 3

Strøget

Copenhagen is a famous shopping destination in the Nordic countries. The street Strøget is one of Europe's longest pedestrian streets with a wealth of shops, from budget-friendly chains to some of the world's most expensive brands. The stretch is 1.1 kilometres long and runs from City Hall Square (Rådhuspladsen) to Kongens Nytorv. When you visit Strøget, there is no doubt that the place is the most famous area for shopping in Copenhagen. Big international brands like Prada, Max Mara, Louis Vuitton, Mulberry, Hermès and Boss are represented here. If you continue along Strøget down towards City Hall Square, where you find shops like H&M, Vero Moda, Ecco, Lego, Pandora, Weekday and Zara.

2 / 3

Illum Shopping Center

ILLUM is Copenhagen's premium department store and offers lots of inspiration for the shopper, gourmet and tourist. The department store is perfectly located in the heart of Copenhagen on the corner between Strøget and Købmagergade - the two major pedestrian streets in the inner city. Illum shopping mall can meet your shopping dreams offering everything from fashion and beauty to home décor and design. As a shopping destination, the department store offers the best Nordic and international brands in fashion, beauty and design. For the quality-conscious foodies, there is the Italian speciality chain, Eataly in the basement and Illum Rooftop with various restaurants and balcony views of Amagertorv, Strøget and Christiansborg.

Danish Butter Cookies

Works by Hans Christian Andersen

H.C. Andersen is without a doubt one of Denmark’s most famous authors and beloved by adults and children alike.
